Policies & Information

  • Service Style: Appetizers are served for every two people to encourage a relaxed, shared dining experience. Main courses are individual portions.
  • Quality Ingredients: We exclusively use fresh vegetables, top-quality meats, Cretan P.D.O. cheeses, and extra virgin Cretan olive oil.
  • Fish Policy: Fish is served filleted (boneless). Dentex or White Grouper (2-3kg) are available upon request based on market availability.
  • Pricing: Prices are subject to change based on seasonal market fluctuations.
